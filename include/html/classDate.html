Detailed Description

Date.

Receives and validates date.

Constructor & Destructor Documentation

Date::Date ( std::string  in_value)

Constructor.

Initializes date and validates its value, following the standards notion(DD/MMM/AAAA), where:

DD is two digits number between 01 to 31, MMM is the month: jan,fev, mar, abr, mai, jun, jul, ago, set, out, nov or dez. AAAA is a year: 2000 to 2099, considering Leap years.
